About Me

Hi I'm Jane! a third year student studying Global Business and Digital Arts at the University of Waterloo. My design approach is grounded in empathy where I strive to understand users deeply and consider diverse perspectives to create meaningful and accessible designs. I also value ambition and using creative problem solving to create digital experiences that resonate with users. I am continually exploring new opportunities to enhance my skills and grow as a designer!
